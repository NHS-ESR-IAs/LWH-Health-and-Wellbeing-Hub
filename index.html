Welcome to the Employee Health and Wellbeing Hub

Your health and wellbeing is important to us. We want you to feel happy, healthy, and productive at work and in your personal life. That's why we have created this website to provide you with information, tips, and resources on various topics related to your physical, mental, and emotional wellbeing.

On this hub, you will find:

  • Articles and videos on topics such as stress management, nutrition, fitness, sleep, mindfulness, and more

Carers

Stress is a normal and inevitable part of life. It can be caused by various factors, such as work, family, health, finances, or personal issues. Stress can affect your mood, energy, productivity, and health. However, stress can also be a positive force that motivates you to overcome challenges and achieve your goals.

The key to managing stress is to recognise its signs, understand its sources, and learn how to cope with it effectively. On this page, you will find:

  • Information and tips on how to identify and reduce stress in your life
  • Strategies and techniques on how to relax and calm your mind and body
  • Resources and tools on how to build resilience and cope with stress in different situations
  • Support and guidance on how to seek help if you feel overwhelmed or stressed out

Finances

Fitness is the ability to perform physical activities that require strength, endurance, flexibility, and coordination. Fitness is not only important for your health and wellbeing, but also for your enjoyment and satisfaction in life. Fitness can help you prevent and manage various chronic conditions, such as heart disease, diabetes, and obesity. Fitness can also boost your mood, energy, confidence, and self-esteem.

On this web page, you will find:

  • Information and tips on how to assess and improve your fitness level, based on your age, gender, and goals
  • Exercises and workouts for different fitness components, such as cardio, strength, balance, and mobility
  • Advice and guidance on how to prevent and recover from injuries, and how to stay safe and motivated while exercising
  • Resources and tools on how to track and measure your fitness progress, and how to set and achieve your fitness goals
  • Support and services on how to access and join fitness facilities, programs, and communities, both online and offline

Mental Health

Mental Health

Mental Health

Counselling is a type of talking therapy that can help you with various emotional and personal issues. Counselling involves having a confidential conversation with a trained professional who will listen to you and help you find ways to cope and improve your situation. Counselling can help you with issues such as:

  • Stress, anxiety, and depression
  • Relationship problems, such as breakups, divorce, or family conflicts
  • Grief and loss, such as bereavement, illness, or redundancy
  • Self-esteem and confidence, such as low self-worth, body image, or identity issues
  • Trauma and abuse, such as domestic violence, sexual assault, or childhood abuse
  • Addiction and recovery, such as alcohol, drugs, gambling, or eating disorders

On this web page, you will find:

  • Information and tips on how to access and benefit from counselling
  • Different types of counselling, such as individual, couples, group, or online counselling
  • How to find and choose a counsellor that suits your needs and preferences
  • How to prepare for and make the most of your counselling sessions
  • How to deal with any challenges or difficulties that may arise during counselling
  • How to evaluate and end your counselling process
